### Security

- HTTPS/TLS encryption
- Server-side secret storage
- Multi-factor authentication on administrative systems
- Least-privilege access
- Secure cloud providers including Plaid and Supabase

### Data Sharing

Information is shared only with service providers required to operate SpendSmart (such as Plaid and Supabase) or when legally required.

We do **not** sell personal information.
